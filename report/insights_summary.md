Amazon Review Sentiment Analysis

Dataset Size:
568,454 Reviews

Key Findings:

- 78.1% reviews are Positive
- 14.4% reviews are Negative
- 7.5% reviews are Neutral

Model Used:
- TF-IDF Vectorization
- Logistic Regression

Model Performance:
Accuracy: 86.7%

Observations:
- Positive reviews dominate the dataset.
- Neutral reviews are harder to classify.
- Logistic Regression performs strongly on positive sentiment detection.
